TRIGGERS

Any Product Card placed near the middle of the page after scrolling down a little bit:

  • Hover On
  • Hover Off
  • Click

Save button that appears in the top left corner when a product card is hovered over:

  • Hover On
  • Hover Off

Tag banner that appears in the top right corner when a product card is hovered over:

  • Hover On
  • Hover Off
  • Click

RULES

Product Card:

  • Hover On:
    • There is an overlay on the image with 30% opacity, darkening the image
    • A save folder appears in the top left corner in a capsule shape, with a folder icon on the left, and the text “save” on the right
    • A blue bookmark shaped tag (with the abbreviation of the tag in two letters, placed towards the bottom of the tag) slides down
    • Cursor changes to finger
  • Hover Off:
    • The overlay disappears and the image brightens to original state
    • The save capsule fades away
    • The bookmark tag banner slides up
    • Finger changes back to cursor
  • Click
    • Goes to/links to a new webpage

Save Button:

  • Hover On:
    • The slightly transparent and blurred black background color of the button darkens (over product images that have a light background)
    • Over product images that have a dark background, the button lightens to a greyish color
  • Hover Off:
    • The button color goes back to its original state

Tag Banner:

  • Hover On:
    • The banner slides down a bit more
  • Hover Off:
    • The banner slides back to original position
  • Click
    • Goes to/links to a new webpage
